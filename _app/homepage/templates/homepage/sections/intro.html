What is WebAuthn?

Welcome to webauthn.io! This site is designed by Duo Labs to test WebAuthn and passkeys. WebAuthn enjoys wide support in all evergreen browsers including Chrome, Safari, and Firefox, and in all modern operating systems including Android, iOS, macOS, and Windows.

For an introduction to WebAuthn and what it can do, check out webauthn.guide. If you're looking to add WebAuthn support to your site then passkeys.dev has you covered. The code for this demo can be found on GitHub.
